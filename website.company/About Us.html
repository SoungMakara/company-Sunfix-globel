Introducing Our Founder & CEO

Mr. Kosol SIENG

Mr. Kosol Sieng is the visionary Founder and Chief Executive Officer of **SUNFIX Consulting**, which he established in **February 2012**. Under his leadership, SUNFIX has grown from its origins in Cambodia to become a leading enterprise software provider with a significant global footprint across Asia-Pacific, the United States, and Europe.

His commitment is centered on delivering not only world-class **ERP technology** from partners like **CUSCEN**, **SAP**, and **Odoo** but also strategic consulting and ethical, trusted service. Mr. Kosol Sieng guides the expert technical and industrial consulting teams at SUNFIX, driving the company’s mission to be the ultimate **End-to-End Partner for Success** for every client.
